A renewed safe and memorable management experience for club members

An exciting project in which I was able to work different disciplines and design approaches. From the deep research for the data collection, both from people outside the club and employees of the company, to the design of the new management tool for club members, through the new design system that will feed all the digital products of the club.

OVERVIEW

FC Barcelona went to the agency with a clear premise. They needed a new experience for their members. The main objective of the tool was that the member could manage their season tickets, regardless of the sport they were attending and could at the same time access the stadium in a totally safe and memorable way.

Scenario analysis

FC Barcelona was going through a time of change in all its digital channels and was looking for the unification of its products.

Problem definition

Designing a simple and intuitive management tool for club members that provides secure and memorable access to the stadium

Methodology

We work by design thinking, where we were able to approach the problem from an empathetic point of view, taking into account the user's needs.

Diseño de la sulución

We designed the entire user experience around a new information architecture and a new visual line, more attractive, modern and connected with the values of the club.

Starting point

For the redesign of this tool, the club provided us with the original user flow to understand where we came from and to know where they did not want to go back.

Desk Research

Through the various interviews we conducted, we were able to analyze the internal culture of the club to understand the context around the decisions that had been made so far, and we also gained valuable insight into the various pain points experienced by external users when interacting with the original application.

Data

Some of the valuable data provided by the club that helped us to know our audience better and understand their needs in order to design a design that always places the user at the center of the decisions.

Empathy map

Through this technique we were able to get to know in detail the behavior of the users and their lifestyle. We analyzed the context, thoughts, actions, needs and problems of both internal and external club members to get a complete picture of our target and to really empathize and connect with them.

Pain points

We discovered key user pain points. Based on these three points, we designed a new user experience where we proposed simple solutions to complex problems.

Impact-Effort Grid.

With this exercise we were able to identify which would be the main functionalities and desires that users wanted to see solved in our design proposal, while we aligned ourselves with the business objectives to know which of them would be truly feasible to include in the MVP.

Visual guidelines

We defined a new visual line more in line with the brand values of the club, more modern and digital thinking in the different platforms where their products are consumed.

Multi-factor authentication

This registration system allow users to prove their identity by providing two factors when logging in, their cell phone and their personal email address. This mechanism linking user access with multiple types of factors makes it more difficult for common threats such as phishing attacks and account transfers to be successful.

Onboarding

We dedicate a special section to onboarding where we explain the new features included in this new version of the application so that the user can become familiar with this new content.

Home

Thanks to this new architecture, the user can quickly access his ticket on game day, while checking the status of his subscription and his accumulated balance. We also contemplate a news area where audiovisual content can be consumed and is renewed every 24 hours.

Seient invers

This is a concept that the club wanted to maintain due to the strong welcome and familiarity that is already established in the membership. When the institution decides to activate the “Reverse Seat”, the user will be notified through a module that is always visible on the home page, explaining how his situation has changed and how to manage his subscription from now on.

Multiple match management

For quick management, we designed the new multiple seat management experience, where users can enter the edit mode to manage as many games as they want at once. The different management options (release, transfer or retrieve) can be changed through a management selector.

Reward section

A more visual and recognizable bonus system, where you can get the details of which matches we have managed and which we have not. We also added an information mode where users can solve their doubts and frequently asked questions.

Management details

A section where you can know the details of the management of each match, as well as the status of such management, that is, you can check if the match has been attended, if it has not been managed or if, for example, it has been transferred to a third party.

Multisport management

One of the requirements most frequently requested by users was the ability to manage their different subscriptions from a single application. With the sport selector, users will be able to do this at any time within the application.

Dinamic tickets

The new dynamic tickets were designed to prevent fraud and counterfeiting. These tickets feature dynamic elements such as a change of color in the background and a prominent strip that moves transversally with the date of the match. In addition, the QR code will only be activated in the moments prior to the match. They also have a code that is automatically generated and updated from time to time.

Multi-profile management

Multi-profile management allows users who have family members included in their app to manage third-party subscriptions in order to centralize all tasks in one place.
