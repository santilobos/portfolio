Education

Full stack development

I fell in love with the world of web development and programming languages watching youtube tutorials to understand how my favorite websites worked. That's why I decided to train and study full stack web development with an intensive course at The Bridge school. Now I keep training remotely in schools like Full Stack Open and Codecademy, to keep my knowledge always updated in terms of technologies and best practices.

Design system master

I have always been interested in how things work around me and how for some reason they are connected to each other. That's why I think I feel so comfortable working on design systems, where each of the parts must to have connection so that the whole can stay alive. That's why I studied a master's degree in Design Systems at Mr Marcel School, where I learned the basics of this discipline.

Product design master

One of my great passions is to solve problems in a creative and functional way. That's why I got into the world of digital product design thanks to the master I did at Mr Marcel design school, where I learned the necessary techniques to design functional digital products based on intentional design decisions that help solve everyday problems and make life easier for users through technology.

Visual design degree

I have always been passionate about design and the power it has to convey concepts and emotions through graphic compositions. That's why I studied design at the University of Malaga, where I learned important theories of information psychology such as Gestalt, or the most relevant thoughts of the Swiss school of design. I am passionate about order and grid and a good typography that provides distinction and character.
